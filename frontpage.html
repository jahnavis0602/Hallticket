Hall Ticket Automation with Integrated QR Codes

Streamlining hall ticket generation and verification with QR code technology.

The Hall Ticket Generator with Integrated QR Code project aims to streamline the process of generating and verifying hall tickets for students, ensuring accuracy and efficiency. By incorporating QR codes, each hall ticket can be easily scanned for validation, reducing the chances of errors during exam check-ins. The system allows admins to upload student data, generate hall tickets in PDF format, and download or print them, with embedded QR codes containing essential student and exam information. The backend is powered by Python and Flask, and data is securely stored in MySQL, providing a reliable platform for managing student records.
